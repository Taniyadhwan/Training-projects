Summary A thundrestorm approaches and three withches convene.They agree to confront the great scot general Macbeth upon his victorious return from a war between scotland and norway .Soon,heroic Macbeth will receive the title of thane of cawdor from King Duncan. However,Macbeth learns from the withches that he is fated for greater things and he will be led down the path of destruction by his unquenchable ambition.
